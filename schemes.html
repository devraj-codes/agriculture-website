All Schemes

6 active central government schemes for farmers

💰
Central Scheme
PM-KISAN Samman Nidhi
Direct income support of ₹6,000 per year to all landholding farmer families, paid in 3 installments of ₹2,000 directly to bank accounts.
Benefit: ₹6,000/year direct transfer
All farmers Direct DBT No income limit
Apply on pmkisan.gov.in
🛡️
Crop Insurance
PM Fasal Bima Yojana
Comprehensive crop insurance against natural calamities, pests, and diseases. Farmers pay only 2% premium for Kharif, 1.5% for Rabi crops.
Benefit: Full crop loss coverage
Kharif & Rabi Low premium Natural calamities
Apply on pmfby.gov.in
💳
Credit Scheme
Kisan Credit Card (KCC)
Provides farmers with timely credit for agricultural needs including crop cultivation, post-harvest expenses, and allied activities at 4% interest rate.
Benefit: Credit up to ₹3 lakh @ 4% p.a.
Short-term credit 4% interest All crops
Apply via Bank/NABARD
🏪
Market Scheme
eNAM — National Market
Electronic national agriculture market connecting 1,000+ mandis online. Farmers can sell produce directly to buyers across India for better prices.
Benefit: Better price discovery & direct sale
Online trading 1000+ mandis Better prices
Register on enam.gov.in
🧪
Central Scheme
Soil Health Card Scheme
Free soil testing and personalized fertilizer recommendations for farmers. Helps reduce input costs and improve crop yield by using the right nutrients.
Benefit: Free soil test + fertilizer advisory
Free testing All farmers Fertilizer advice
Check soilhealth.dac.gov.in
🌿
Central Scheme
Paramparagat Krishi Vikas Yojana
Promotes organic farming through cluster-based approach. Farmers get financial assistance for organic certification, input production, and marketing.
Benefit: ₹50,000/hectare over 3 years
Organic farming Cluster-based Certification support
Visit pgsindia-ncof.gov.in
